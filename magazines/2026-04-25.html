Craft Down 5.1%—Buy The Dip?

The BA's definitive 2025 numbers are in: production fell 5.1%, 481 breweries closed, and the total count dropped to 9,578—but craft's volume share of total beer actually ticked up to 13.3% as overall beer fell harder. BA economist Matt Gacioch is calling 2026 a potential 'buying the dip' moment for new entrants willing to build differentiated brands.

Spirits RTDs Up 25.7%—Blueprint For Growth

NIQ data confirms RTDs across all segments hit $13.6 billion in 2025 and now represent 12.7% of total bev-alc off-premise dollars, with spirits-based formats up 25.7% and offsetting roughly $650M in core spirits declines. Analysts compare RTD's trajectory to early craft beer—a decade of growth, then consolidation and acquisition.
