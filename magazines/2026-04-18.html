Craft Beer's Worst Two Years, Mapped

The BA's annual production report confirms craft volume cratered 5.1% in 2025—the steepest non-pandemic drop on record—with 1,072 closures over two years against only 817 openings. Taprooms and brewpubs held up better than distribution-heavy models, which is a direct signal for Mayport to double down on its on-premise experience and local loyalty.

⚡RELEVANT TO YOU — Microbreweries took the worst hit (-8.9%); taproom-first models like Mayport's outperformed. Know your relative position before CBC week.
