RTDs Hit $13.6B, Spirits Lead the Charge

RTDs now represent 12.7% of all bev-alc off-premise dollar sales, reaching $13.6 billion in 2025 per NIQ, with spirits-based RTDs up 25.7% and offsetting $650M in straight-spirits declines. Distribution power is shifting fast: major suppliers are dumping RNDC for Reyes Beverage Group and regional beer distributors to get RTD products into more hands.

⚡RELEVANT TO YOU — Florida's spirits-RTD distribution landscape is in flux — beer distributors in the Southeast are actively picking up spirits-RTD lines, which means competition for your distributor's attention (and cold box space) is intensifying.

Boston Beer's $175M Can Catastrophe

A jury handed Ardagh Metal Packaging USA a $175.5 million verdict against Boston Beer for allegedly failing to purchase contractual minimum volumes of aluminum cans from 2021 to 2025.
