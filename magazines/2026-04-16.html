Boston Beer Owes $175M In Can Lawsuit

A jury sided with Ardagh Metal Packaging against Boston Beer for allegedly failing to purchase contracted minimum volumes of aluminum cans from 2021–2025, resulting in a $175.5M verdict. This is a cautionary tale for any brewery locking into long-term supply contracts — volume flexibility clauses are non-negotiable in a volatile demand environment.
