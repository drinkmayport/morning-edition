Craft Platform Ditches Brewery, Eyes Pivot

Evergreen Collective — parent of Lord Hobo and Lone Pine — is shutting down its own brewing floor and shifting production to Isle Brewers Guild in Pawtucket, RI as it chases a hospitality and beyond-beer future. This is a live case study in what contract brewing plus brand diversification looks like for a mid-size craft platform navigating a brutal market — and a signal that owning your own kettles is no longer a prerequisite for surviving.

⚡RELEVANT TO YOU — Direct strategic parallel for Mayport: the calculus of self-production vs. contract brewing is a decision every growing SE craft startup will face. Watch this model closely.

With a Congressional ban on intoxicating hemp products set for November 12, THC drink brands are paradoxically expanding retail footprints and distribution partnerships — Delta-9 THC drink sales were up 148% YoY in the 52 weeks ending February 22 per SPINS.
